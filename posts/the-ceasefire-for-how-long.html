The ceasefire. For how long?

Will we return to the war soon?

The USA and Iran reached a ceasefire. Right before the end of Trump's ultimatum to destroy everything within the Islamic Republic.

A whole civilization will die tonight, never to be brought back again.

That's what he said. An hour before the US President accepted the Pakistani/Iranian deal. Probably a very fragile one, but we'll see.

It actually looks like Trump is tired already. I can see that his dream of an immediate fall of the regime didn't come true. True, the ayatollahs strengthened their positions drastically after the 12-Day War with Israel. After the death of Ali Khamenei, the de-facto war junta controls Iran — the collective dictatorship of the IRGC. The most dangerous type of dictatorship.

Will the regime stay? Once again, we'll see. My prediction is that it's impossible to overthrow them without a ground operation. We can't destroy them using only aircraft. That's not how it works.

The new round, in my opinion, is inevitable. The question is only when it will occur.
