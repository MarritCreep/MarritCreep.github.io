The peace deal to be signed. Catastrophe?

After another Dahieh strike, Trump managed to make a deal with the Iranians. And it looks like very bad news for us.

The Deal with the Islamic Republic of Iran is now complete. Congratulations to all! I hereby fully authorize the toll free opening of the Strait of Hormuz, and, simultaneously herewith, authorize the immediate removal of the United States Naval blockade. Ships of the World, start your engines. Let the oil flow! President DONALD J. TRUMP

This is the post from Trump's Truth Social account. And it actually enraged me.

Let's start from the beginning. Last night, Israel hit Dahieh once again, and Iran once again threatened to respond to Israel. But it didn't happen, because Trump "made a deal."

We don't know the details yet, but it remains to be seen what the US President agreed to. Iranian sources are saying something absolutely crazy about it. Billions of dollars, the lifting of sanctions, a fee for passage through the Strait of Hormuz. If it's true…

We're in deep shit. Sorry, but most likely we are.

The very important point is that, from the start of the negotiations, the two sides (the US and Iran) didn't become closer. Not even by an inch. Religious fanatics are not willing to cooperate or to make concessions.

Well, we'll see. I'm hoping for the best. But it seems like the only choice is between two options — bad and terrible.
