The Knesset to be dissolved. What does that mean?

No majority for non-conscription of Haredim — no Knesset.

The expected event.

After the failure to adopt the law for non-conscription of Haredim, ultra-Orthodox Jews threatened to dissolve the Knesset.

We no longer have confidence in Netanyahu... From this point forward, we will do only what is good for Haredi Judaism and the yeshiva world. We must act to dissolve the Knesset as soon as possible. As far as we are concerned, the concept of a political 'bloc' no longer exists.

This is what Rabbi Dov Landau said. Taking into account the inevitability of dissolution, a law that would do that was introduced by both the opposition and the coalition. Every MK who attended this meeting (110 out of 120) voted in favor of this law.

What now? Well, it means that early elections will be called. As I understand, somewhere between the beginning of September and the beginning of October.

Haredim want a certain date, because they will be able to make as many of their voters as possible come to vote. On the other hand, Netanyahu also doesn't want the elections to be held on October 27, as was originally planned (there is a strong link between this date and the October 7 catastrophe).

I bet that the elections will be held at the beginning or the middle of September.

Be ready. Vote for who your mind says to. Vote rationally. Don't let yourself be swayed by propaganda from both sides, and check every statement made by politicians.
