Israel War

The Iranian strike on Israel. The war resumes?

Iran struck Israel as a response to our strike on Dahieh. What's happening?

We are striking them very hard, and we know that Hezbollah is on the run. We will not allow firing at our territory or our communities, and we will act accordingly.

That's what the Israeli prime minister Benjamin Netanyahu said after the strike in Dahieh, a suburb of the Lebanese capital, Beirut. By the way, Dahieh is the unofficial capital of Hezbollah, with an overwhelming majority of Shias living there.

Then, Iran threatened to respond to the "Zionist aggression." At the end of the day, Iran launched several ballistic missiles. All of them were shot down, but it was a clear signal. And it's absolutely clear that Iran is trying to tie the ceasefire with the Islamic Republic to the Hezbollah war.

Trump, once again, declared a ceasefire, trying to convince Netanyahu to refrain from striking back. We didn't refrain, showing Iran that our intentions are serious enough. Looks like this round ended in a draw.
