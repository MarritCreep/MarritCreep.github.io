Who am I?

March 24, 2026

Hey!

My name is Vladislav (or Vlad, whatever). I'm a part-time independent journalist, writing the things I am passionate about. This includes the news articles and journalistic investigations.

I was born in Russia, in Volgograd (formerly Stalingrad), which you may have heard about for the bloodiest battle in human history.

Unfortunately (or, perhaps, even fortunately), our short tsar (I'm sure he considers himself some kind of emperor) made me and my family move to Israel after I finished 9th grade. We understood that there would be no improvement in the condition of our state.

However, it wasn't bad. In contrast, it was, and it is a great experience. In Israel, I got an opportunity to fulfil my true potential, not being afraid of being imprisoned :P
